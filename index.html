Our Services

At NewCosmetics, we're more than just a bridge between cosmetic manufacturers and demanding markets. We provide a spectrum of bespoke services tailored to meet the unique needs of our partners.

Here's a glimpse into what we offer:

Market Insights & Analysis: Armed with in-depth research and a pulse on current trends, we provide actionable insights to help manufacturers align their offerings with European market demands.

Product Showcasing & Branding: Our dedicated platform ensures that your products are presented in the best light, resonating with the aesthetics and values of premium European clientele.

Supply Chain Management: From sourcing raw materials to delivering the finished product, we streamline every step to ensure efficiency, quality, and timely delivery.

Digital Solutions: Leveraging the rich experience of our e-commerce expert, Nemanja, we offer cutting-edge digital solutions, ensuring a seamless online experience for our partners and customers.

Sustainable Strategy Consultation: In line with global sustainability trends, we offer consultation services to help brands transition to eco-friendly practices without compromising on quality.

Customized Marketing Campaigns: Drawing from our deep understanding of the European beauty landscape, we craft marketing strategies that speak directly to your target audience, amplifying brand visibility and engagement.

Partnership Facilitation: Our extensive network across Europe means we can connect manufacturers with the right distributors, retailers, and influencers, setting the stage for fruitful collaborations.
